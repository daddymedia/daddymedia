Frequently Asked Questions

Is Daddy Media free to use?

Yes! Creating a profile, browsing jobs, applying, and joining communities is completely free for users.

How do I apply for a job or internship?

You must be logged in to apply. Once logged in, click on any job listing and use the "Apply Now" button.

Can I post in the community without an account?

No. While anyone can read community posts, you must create a free account to post, like, or comment to ensure a safe environment.
